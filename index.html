Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will berume your now favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the Focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus mure for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon halsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top to ounces Brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not bemessary)
  • 11/2 cups frish or froarn cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the doogh, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the boost when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour.
